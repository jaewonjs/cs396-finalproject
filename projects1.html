Zoom Bullies

Winter 2021

Problem Description: Have you ever been in a Zoom meeting where someone is dominating the conversation? Even worse, has someone spent the whole meeting being unproductive or making inappropriate comments? This was the problem we wanted to tackle.

Tech Stack: We used ML models to create a live transcript that detected when participants made negative statements. Then we collected data throughout the meeting and once the meeting was ended, we sent each participant a report about their meeting behavior.
